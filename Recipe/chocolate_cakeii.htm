Milk-free Chocolate Cake II     
Submitted by:  Juliane

Milk-free Egg-free Wheat-free Peanut-free
Soy-free Nut-free  Other 

Ingredients:

3 cups flour
2 cups sugar
6 tbsp cocoa
2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
2 tbsp white vinegar
¾ cup canola oil
2 tsp vanilla
2 cups hot water

Directions:

  1. Sift first 5 ingredients together in an increased 9x13 pan. ( I don’t sift and it comes out fine)
  2. Make 3 wells and in each hole place the next 3 ingredients.
  3. Pour 2 cups of water over top.  Mix with a fork or whisk.
  4. Bake @ 350 for 30-35 minutes.
  5. Tips…. When mixing I use a spoon in the corners to make sure all the wet mixes with the dry.  After mixing put in oven immediately.  One time I got distracted and my cake was like cement.

Variation… Instead of adding the cocoa add cinnamon and nutmeg.  My kids love it!
