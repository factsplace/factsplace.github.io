Cowboy Cookies      
Submitted by:  Sandy Iuppa

Milk-free Egg-free Wheat-free Peanut-free
Soy-free Nut-free  Other 

Ingredients:

1 1/3 cup flour
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1 1/3 cup rolled oats or quick oats
3/4Cup choc chips (Tops or Wegs)
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon vanilla
1/2 cup margarine or oil
1 egg

Directions:

Mix all ingredients together well. Mixture will be kinda dry. Drop round balls of mixture on to greased cookie sheet. Bake 350* for 11 to 13 minutes
