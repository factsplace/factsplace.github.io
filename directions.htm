Directions to Fairport United Methodist Church
31 West Church Street, Fairport

From 490:

1. Take the exit for Fairport, Route 31F (Exit 25).
2. Head east towards Fairport.
3. Cross the Erie Canal and pass Perinton Park. Continue through the light by Potter Park and the church (a large brick building) will be on your right.
4. Enter the church’s parking lot from South Avenue.

From Route 250 (North of 31F)

1. Head south on Route 250 and cross the Erie Canal lift bridge.
2. At the intersection of Route 250 and Church Street (31F), turn right onto West Church Street.
3. Turn at the next left on to South Avenue to enter the church’s parking lot.

From Route 250 (South of 31F)

1. Head north on Route 250.
2. At the intersection of Route 250 and Church Street (31F), turn left onto West Church Street.
3. Turn at the next left on to South Avenue to enter the church’s parking lot.

If you need more specific directions or if you are coming from another direction, please contact Joy Leinenbach at (585) 264-9033 or at ejleinenbach@usadatanet.net  for assistance.
